Shop

Welcome to Bitter Sweet Tattoo, one of Belfast’s most exciting and modern tattoo studio’s located in north Belfast’s Crumlin road. Bitter Sweet has been opened since 2012 and is now in its second shop. Here at Bitter Sweet we offer all types of styles concepts of tattoo’s including fine realism to bold Japanese work, old school and new school but most of all we specialise in custom tattoos for you to have the most unique, original piece of body art possible. It is our main aim to achieve the highest quality and creative designs possible for each and every client!!!!! So for quality tattoo work at a very reasonable prices contact shop manger and tattoo artist Michael Ferris or call in to Bitter Sweet Tattoo, Belfast’s latest studio…

owner and head artist

Shop Owner and head tattoo artist Michael Ferris is the driving force behind Bittersweet tattoo. Michael has been tattooing for many years, working in shops all over Belfast with people coming from far and wide just to be tattooed by Michael. After working in many shops Michael decided it was time to open up Bittersweet Tattoo and has turned into one of Belfast’s main tattoo studios.
